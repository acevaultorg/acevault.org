Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Short version

We collect only what we need to run the product. We don't sell your data. We use privacy-first analytics that don't track you across the web. You can contact us to see, export, or delete your data at any time.

What we collect

When you use AceVault products, we may collect:

  • Account information (email, name) if you sign up
  • Content you create or upload inside the product
  • Aggregate, anonymous analytics about how our sites are used
  • Server logs for debugging and abuse prevention

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first, cookieless analytics tool. No personal data is collected or shared. No ads. No cross-site tracking.

Cookies

Our marketing site uses no tracking cookies. Products may use a small number of essential cookies (for example, to keep you logged in). Each product's own privacy notice explains details.

Your rights

You can request a copy, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@acevault.org.

Changes

When this policy changes in a material way, we will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notify users in the product.
